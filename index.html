About Me

Hello, I'm Markus

I'm an FX & Creature FX Technical Director with experience across episodics and commercials, passionate about technical problem solving and tool development.

My work focuses on developing sophisticated effects simulations, creature work, and dynamic FX that push the boundaries of what's possible in visual storytelling. I specialize in Houdini, Python, VEX, and various industry-standard tools including Nuke, Arnold, and USD.

I graduated from the University of California, San Diego with a B.A. in Visual Arts: Media and a B.S. in Psychology. When I'm not creating VFX, you can find me playing board games, hiking, watching Formula 1, or listening to podcasts.
